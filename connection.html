Connection to authorities and other resources

We have connected our data to other related items, in particular to the same authorites for names (people, places, subjects) and to other resources, related because of the sharing of one of the features of the object, creating semantic associations. In details, check RDF/XML files of:

1) PEOPLE - Napoleon: www.semanticweb.org/waterlod/Napoleon

2) PLACE - Rochefort: www.semanticweb.org/waterlod/Rochefort

3) CONCEPT/SUBJECT - Surrender: www.semanticweb.org/waterlod/Napoleon_surrender


1) PEOPLE - Napoleon: www.semanticweb.org/waterlod/Napoleon

owl:sameAs rdf:resource="https://viaf.org/viaf/106964661/"



"owl:sameAs", a property of OWL Ontology, is connected to "https://viaf.org/viaf/106964661/". This means that "www.semanticweb.org/waterlod/Napoleon", the URI of Napoleon, and "https://viaf.org/viaf/106964661/", coming from VIAF, an international authority file,share features. In this case they both concern the person Napoleon.

owl:sameAs rdf:resource="http://dbpedia.org/page/Napoleon"



"owl:sameAs", a property of OWL Ontology, is connected to " http://dbpedia.org/page/Napoleon". This means that "www.semanticweb.org/waterlod/Napoleon", the URI of Napoleon, and "http://dbpedia.org/page/Napoleon", coming from DBpedia, a website containing a knowledge-base, share features. In this case they both concern the person Napoleon.

owl:differentFrom rdf:resource="http://dbpedia.org/page/Napoleon_III"



"owl:differentFrom", a property of OWL Ontology, is connected to "http://dbpedia.org/page/Napoleon_III". This means that "www.semanticweb.org/waterlod/Napoleon", the URI of Napoleon, and "http://dbpedia.org/page/Napoleon_III", coming from DBpedia, a website containing a knowledge-base, express totally different concepts. In this case Napoleon is different from Napoleon III, or better, Napoleon and Napoleon III are not the same person.
