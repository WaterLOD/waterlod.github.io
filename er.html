E/R Model

This model, called E/R model, relates the central idea "Battle of Waterloo" to the person "Napoleon Bonaparte", the place "Waterloo", the date "1815" and the concept "Defeat".

In these model we have:

  • Entities, which are represented as rectangles
  • Relationships, which are represented as rhombus
